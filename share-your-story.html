The Ground Rules

Real stories, fully anonymized.

The same standards we apply to our own Claim Files apply to yours.

What to include

  • What happened, in your own words. Don't worry about polish; we edit everything.
  • The turning point: what cracked it open or turned it around
  • The lesson another employer or claims pro should take from it

What to leave out

  • Names, employers, claim numbers, or anything that identifies a real person or company
  • Anything confidential or privileged from a current or former employer
  • Anything from litigation that is still active

Every published story is edited and anonymized to our standards, and clearly credited as a "From the Field" contribution. You choose whether to be named or stay anonymous.
