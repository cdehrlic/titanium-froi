The Claim Lifecycle

What happens the moment an injury occurs.

1

Report

The injury is captured in full through the WC Reporting portal — employee, incident, worksite & accident location, medical status, and evidence — the same day it happens.

2

Screen & prioritize

Built-in red-flag detection scores the claim for fraud and severity, so questionable and high-cost claims get attention immediately.

3

Investigate & document

We collect e-signed witness and claimant statements, preserve evidence, and build a defensible, court-ready record.

4

Manage & contain cost

We coordinate with carriers and TPAs, challenge reserves, scrutinize medical costs, and pursue subrogation and third-party recovery.

5

Return to work

We drive return-to-work and light-duty so employees recover sooner and indemnity cost drops.

6

Prevent the next one

Root-cause analysis and corrective-action tracking close the loop so the same injury doesn't happen again.
