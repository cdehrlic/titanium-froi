About CompShield

We take the employer's side.

CompShield is the workers' compensation claims defense arm of Titanium Defense Group — built for the organizations that carry the risk and pay the bill.

Our Mission

Protect employers from the true cost of claims.

Workers' comp is one of the largest controllable costs most organizations have — and one of the least managed. Too many employers hand their claims to a carrier and hope for the best, absorbing fraud, inflated reserves, and missed recoveries they never even see.

CompShield exists to change that. We treat every claim as something to be reported precisely, investigated thoroughly, and defended aggressively — so you pay what's fair and not a dollar more.

🎯Precise
Complete, defensible documentation on every claim
🔎Thorough
Fraud and cost scrutinized, not assumed away
🛡️On your side
Always working for the employer, never the claim
The Defense Philosophy

Good defense starts before the claim does.

The best time to win a workers' comp claim is the moment the injury is reported. Our whole approach is built to capture the truth early and hold the line from there.

⏱️

Report early

Same-day, structured reporting closes the gaps that questionable claims depend on.

🔬

Investigate everything

Every claim is screened for fraud and severity — the questionable ones get contested, not paid.

💵

Never overpay

Reserves, medical costs, and carrier handling are all held accountable to the real number.

Part of Titanium Defense Group

CompShield is a division of Titanium Defense Group, focused exclusively on workers' compensation. That specialization means deeper expertise, faster response, and a team that does this — and only this — every day.
