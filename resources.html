How to fight a WC claim

Report fast, document everything, gather statements while memories are fresh, and screen for red flags. The claims you contest early are the ones you win.

📉

Lower your experience mod

Frequency hurts more than severity. Cut small repeat claims, return people to work, and dispute what doesn't belong — your mod follows your losses down.

🔄

Build a return-to-work program

Light-duty roles get people back sooner and slash indemnity — the most expensive part of any claim. It's the highest-ROI move most employers skip.
