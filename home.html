The Problem

Workers' comp costs are quietly draining your bottom line.

Late reporting, weak documentation, and unchallenged claims turn every injury into a bigger number than it should be. Most employers never see the fraud, the missed subrogation, or the reserves that never come back down.

⏱️

Late, sloppy reporting

Every day a claim goes unreported raises its cost and its odds of dispute. Gaps in documentation hand the advantage to the other side.

🚩

Unchallenged fraud

Suspicious claims sail through because no one is trained to spot the red flags or gather the statements that would contest them.

📈

Runaway cost & reserves

Inflated reserves, missed return-to-work windows, and unrecovered third-party costs compound into higher premiums year after year.
